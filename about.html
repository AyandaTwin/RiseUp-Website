About Us

RiseUp Youth Foundation is a Johannesburg-based non-profit organisation established in 2018. The organisation focuses on empowering young people by providing access to digital skills training, mentorship programmes, and entrepreneurship development opportunities.

Our mission is to equip young individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to succeed in today’s competitive world. We believe that every young person deserves an opportunity to reach their full potential, regardless of their background.

Our vision is to build a future where youth are empowered, self-sufficient, and actively contributing to their communities.

Since its establishment, the organisation has positively impacted many young individuals by providing them with valuable skills and opportunities for growth.
